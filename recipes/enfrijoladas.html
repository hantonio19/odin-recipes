Tortillas Covered in Beans and Cheese

This recipe is a quick and simple one. We start with opeing our canned bean paste. We follow that by placing it on a skillet to refry. We follow that by dipping our tortillas that have been heated up into the pan and serve with drizled cheese on top.

Ingredients

  • canned refried beans
  • cheese
  • tortilla
  • oil

Steps

  1. open your refried beans
  2. add oil to a pan
  3. add beans to the pan
  4. dip tortillas in the pan
  5. serve on a plate
  6. break cheese on top
