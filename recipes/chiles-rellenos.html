Chiles Rellenos

This recipe is of chiles caifornia that are stuffed with queso fresco and dipped in whipped eggs to create a fluffy egg outter.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • flower
  • chile california
  • queso fresco
  • oil

Steps

  1. Beat some eggs until you have stiff peaks
  2. place chiles on the stove until chared
  3. clean the char off the chiles and clean the inside
  4. stuff qith queso fresco
  5. dip the chile in flour making sure it is all covered
  6. shake off excess flower and dip in egg
  7. place in oil until egg is cooked and cheese is melted
  8. serve
